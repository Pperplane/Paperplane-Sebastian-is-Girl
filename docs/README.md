**Sebastian to Sabrina: Sebastian is a Girl** is a [Stardew Valley](http://stardewvalley.net/) mod that uses both C# and [Content Patcher](https://github.com/Pathoschild/StardewMods/tree/develop/ContentPatcher) to turn Sebastian, an NPC within the game into a girl. Every assets used to this are credited to its respective owner and authors.

### **❓ Why Create this Mod?**
___
So we credited a lot of author, so the Mod actually exist, right? Why did I, Paperplane, created this mod anyway? So the reason behind the creation of this mod is that, while I was playing with my friend, we had a sudden thought of:

*"What if Sebastian is a Girl?"*

So We searched stuff on [Nexus Mod](nexusmods.com/stardewvalley) that does exactly what we thought, then my friend got interested on this mod created by **[AdarinnSinner](https://www.nexusmods.com/profile/AdarinSinner)**, the catch is that, it uses a rather old way of modding, which is completely overwriting game files, which is not the standard now since we already have system like SMAPI and Content Patcher, noticing that the mod is almost 9-years old around the time that we saw it, I had a sudden idea of:

*"What if I tried to port this mod into Content Patcher, making it more accessible and less risky to use?"*

So as an Artist myself, of course I contacted AdarinnSinner for their approval of usage of their assets, fortunately they agreed upon it as long as I don't make money out of it. Of course I also contacted other authors for their approval before continue working on this mod, and all of them agreed. So to all of the Authors and Artist who agreed upon my request, I thank you all for allowing me to work on this Mod with the use of your Assets!

### **ℹ️ About This Mod**
___
This is a mod for people who want to add a twist to one of the Valley's Character, a literal twist. Experience the Valley with **Sabrina**, sister of **Maru**, and one of the daughters of **Robin the Carpenter** and **Demetrius the Scientist**. 

This mod basically turns **Sebastian**, one of the Stardew Valley character into a girl named **Sabrina**.
